# Public Roadmap

This roadmap is intentionally conservative. It describes product direction without promising ship dates.

## Live / Implemented

- Sweden renter flow.
- City-first research start.
- Letter preview, locked contact proof, checkout, unlock, plaintext export, and retry flow.
- Three public package levels that unlock the full contact list.
- Admin-managed discount codes.
- Weekly internal analytics reporting.
- Renter-first verified recipient-address concept for landlords.

## Private Beta / Validation

- U.S. market validation.
- Country-code propagation across run start, preview, checkout, and restore paths.
- Market-specific discovery language, pricing, portal exclusion, and beta gating.
- Offline fixture-backed validation for expansion work.

## Planned / Under Review

- Additional country packs after validation.
- More public proof artifacts such as screenshots and evaluation summaries.
- Broader regression coverage for market expansion and checkout-return edge cases.
- Public changelog once release milestones stabilize.

## Explicitly Not Public In This Release

- Scheduled refresh as a public add-on.
- Source contributions through this mirror.
- Open-source release of code, prompts, ranking logic, datasets, or contact intelligence.
