# FAQ

## Is Hyral open source?

No. This is a public review mirror with non-sensitive product documentation only.

## Can I copy or reuse the idea, docs, or product?

No permission is granted to copy, modify, redistribute, host, resell, or use the work in a competing product or service without explicit written permission. See `NOTICE.md`.

## Does this repository contain the app?

No. It contains public-facing documentation only. Source code, prompts, ranking logic, datasets, infrastructure, and internal docs are excluded.

## What market is live?

Sweden is the live baseline market in the private product repository. U.S. support is beta validation, not public live. Other configured countries should be treated as planning or beta configuration unless separately announced.

## Does Hyral send emails automatically?

No. Hyral prepares the letter and contact batches. The renter sends outreach manually from their own email client.

## Do higher packages hide contacts?

No. The public package model unlocks the full contact list. Higher tiers sell ordering and guidance, not artificial withholding of the basic contact list.

## Is scheduled refresh available?

Not as a public add-on in the current release.

## Can landlords pay for ranking?

No. The verified recipient-address concept is renter-first and manually reviewed. It can identify the right recipient route but does not buy ranking, remove other discovered routes, or create pay-to-win placement.
